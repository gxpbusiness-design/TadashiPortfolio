About

Crafting stories that move people.

Tadashi is a Los Angeles-based filmmaker, producer, and creative director with a lens for the visceral and the real. His work spans psychological thrillers, music videos, live events, brand development, and podcast production — always driven by a singular purpose: to make work that means something.

With comprehensive production experience across every role — from storyboarding to post-production — Tadashi brings a complete creative vision and the technical range to realize it.

From raising $3K to independently produce a psychological horror short, to serving as key creative on projects surpassing 254K views, Tadashi has built a reputation for delivering exceptional work at every scale.
